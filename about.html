Hi! my name is Gricel.

I'm a Graphic Designer & Web Developer, born and raise in the Dominican Republic, currently living in Atlanta, Georgia.
I've always found a passion on creating things. Back when I was in middle school I was in Art class and one of our final projects was to create an original painting, everyone in my class went for a landscape type of painting, but I wanted something that would challenge me into painting something that would be much more than a sky and mountains or a beach. So I remember my godfather had a photoshoot session of his little dog with funny clothes and I went for it. I created a painting that my teacher was so proud of, and I enjoyed every minute I got to work on it.

Hey! I'm no Leonardo da Vinci but my this little dog painting was a work of art for a 10 year old.

Anyways, the years went by and that hunger for being able to create stuff kept being in me. I kept trying to learn new stuff, watching Youtube Videos but I never really got the chance of work towards my goal until I came to live here in the U.S and got the chance to enroll into a Graphic Design & Web Development class that has taught me so much and helped me redefine everything I know.
Now I'm just eager to dive into the Graphic Design world and be able to bring my future clients' ideas into life!
